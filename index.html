ABOUT ME

Born and raised in South Africa, I am a multi-skilled UX/UI & Graphic Designer, based in Atlanta, GA. When I run into bad UX, I can’t help but do something about it, often going above and beyond to redesign the entire experience.

I have a unique ability to shape my designs into compelling visuals that help to support the narrative I'm trying to communicate.

I have extensive design experience in the Greater Atlanta area and have focused on continuously improving myself through various degrees, including a certificate in UX/UI Design from Georgia Institute of Technology, an MBA from Keller Graduate School of Management and a BFA in Graphic Design from The Art Institute of Atlanta.
